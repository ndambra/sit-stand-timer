Stand:Sit Ratio Timer

Have a standing desk? Need to know how long you should stand for? Well, we don't have those answers but........ We've read that it's good to follow a 1:1, 2:1, or, 3:1 stand-to-sit ratio in a given working day. That's a lot of math to figure out, extra work and time you could, and should, be spending on your actual job. Or pretending to do your actual job (Hey, we aren't here to judge).

We all got into the standing game to lead a healthy life style, we find it comfortable, someone told us it was healthy, etc. Whatever the reason we are here to help (kind of)! Simply enter your target working hours, the ratio you want, and just start the timer whenever you are sitting, and the tool will let you know how much of your remaining day you should spend standing
